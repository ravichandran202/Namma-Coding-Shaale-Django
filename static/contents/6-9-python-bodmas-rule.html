BODMAS Rule

BODMAS is an acronym that represents the order of operations in mathematical expressions.

B - Brackets (), O - Orders **, D - Division /, M - Multiplication *, A - Addition +, S - Subtraction -

Operation Symbol Priority Associativity
Parentheses () 1 (Highest) Left to Right
Exponent ** 2 Right to Left
Multiplication * 3 Left to Right
Division /
Floor Division //
Modulus %
Addition + 4 Left to Right
Subtraction -
  1. Parentheses ()

    Highest Priority, Left-to-Right

    Operations inside parentheses are always evaluated first.

    # Example 1: Forcing a different order
result = (10 - 2) * 5
print(result)
 Output
    40
    Step-by-Step Evaluation
    1. Parentheses first: (10 - 2) = 8
    2. Multiplication: 8 * 5 = 40

    Without parentheses: 10 - 2 * 5 = 10 - 10 = 0

    # Example 2: Nested parentheses
result = ((2 + 3) * (6 - 4)) ** 2
print(result)
 Output
    100
    Step-by-Step Evaluation
    1. Innermost parentheses: (2 + 3) = 5
    2. Next parentheses: (6 - 4) = 2
    3. Multiplication: 5 * 2 = 10
    4. Exponent: 10 ** 2 = 100
  2. Exponent **

    Priority 2, Right-to-Left Associativity

    This means 2 ** 3 ** 2 is evaluated as 2 ** (3 ** 2), not (2 ** 3) ** 2. 

    # Example 1: Basic exponent
result = 2 ** 3
print(result) 
 Output
 8
    Step-by-Step Evaluation
    1. Exponent: 2 ** 3 = 2 * 2 * 2 = 8
    # Example 2: Right associativity
result = 2 ** 3 ** 2
print(result)
 Output
 512
    Step-by-Step Evaluation
    1. Right associativity: 3 ** 2 = 9 first
    2. Then: 2 ** 9 = 512
    # Example 3: Forced left associativity
result_left = (2 ** 3) ** 2
print(result_left) 
 Output
 64
    Step-by-Step Evaluation
    1. Parentheses first: (2 ** 3) = 8
    2. Exponent: 8 ** 2 = 64
  3. Multiplication *, Division /, Floor Division //, Modulus %

    Priority 3, Left-to-Right Associativity

    These operations have equal priority and are evaluated from left to right.

    # Multiplication *
result1 = 10 * 3

# Division / (Float Division)
result2 = 10 / 3

# Floor Division // (Integer Division)
result3 = 10 // 3

# Modulus % (Remainder)
result4 = 10 % 3

print(result1)
print(result2) 
print(result3)
print(result4)
 Output
 30 
3.3333333333333335
3 
1
    Step-by-Step Evaluation
    • 10 * 3 = 30 (Multiplication)
    • 10 / 3 = 3.333... (Float Division)
    • 10 // 3 = 3 (How many whole times 3 fits in 10)
    • 10 % 3 = 1 (Remainder of 10 ÷ 3)
    # Left-to-right evaluation
result = 16 / 4 * 2 % 5
print(result)
 Output
 3.0
    Step-by-Step Evaluation
    1. Leftmost division: 16 / 4 = 4.0
    2. Multiplication: 4.0 * 2 = 8.0
    3. Modulus: 8.0 % 5 = 3.0
  4. Addition + and Subtraction -

    Priority 4 (Lowest), Left-to-Right Associativity

    # Addition +
result1 = 5 + 7

# Subtraction -
result2 = 5 - 7

print(result1, result2)
    # Left-to-right evaluation
result = 10 - 5 + 3
print(result)
    Step-by-Step Evaluation
    1. Leftmost subtraction: 10 - 5 = 5
    2. Addition: 5 + 3 = 8

    Note: It is NOT 10 - (5 + 3) = 2. Subtraction and addition have equal priority, so we go left to right.

    Output 
    12 -2
8
Putting It All Together: A Complex Example

Let's solve a complex expression step-by-step according to BODMAS:

result = 8 + (5 * 3) ** 2 / 5 - 20 // 6
print(result)
Step-by-Step Evaluation
  1. Parentheses: (5 * 3) = 15
  2. Expression becomes: 8 + 15 ** 2 / 5 - 20 // 6
  3. Exponent: 15 ** 2 = 225
  4. Expression becomes: 8 + 225 / 5 - 20 // 6
  5. Division (Leftmost): 225 / 5 = 45.0
  6. Expression becomes: 8 + 45.0 - 20 // 6
  7. Floor Division: 20 // 6 = 3 (6×3=18, remainder 2)
  8. Expression becomes: 8 + 45.0 - 3
  9. Addition (Left to Right): 8 + 45.0 = 53.0
  10. Subtraction: 53.0 - 3 = 50.0
Output 
50.0
Practice Exercise

Evaluate the following expression step by step:

expression = 15 - 3 * (4 + 2) // 5 ** 2

Try to solve it manually first, then check your answer below.

Step-by-Step Solution
  1. Parentheses: (4 + 2) = 6
  2. Expression: 15 - 3 * 6 // 5 ** 2
  3. Exponent: 5 ** 2 = 25
  4. Expression: 15 - 3 * 6 // 25
  5. Multiplication: 3 * 6 = 18
  6. Expression: 15 - 18 // 25
  7. Floor Division: 18 // 25 = 0
  8. Expression: 15 - 0
  9. Subtraction: 15 - 0 = 15
result = 15 - 3 * (4 + 2) // 5 ** 2
print(result)  # Output: 15
