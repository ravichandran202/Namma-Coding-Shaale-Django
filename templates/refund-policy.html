Refund Policy

Our guidelines for course fee refunds and cancellations

Introduction

At Namma Coding Shaale, we are committed to providing quality educational experiences through our coding and technology programs. This Refund Policy outlines the terms under which refunds may be issued for our courses and services.

1. Course Enrollment and Fees

  • All course fees must be paid in full at the time of registration unless a payment plan has been arranged and agreed upon in writing.
  • Fees once paid are non-transferable to another person or course.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds will be issued only under the following conditions:

a. Cancellation Before Course Start

If you cancel your enrollment at least 7 days before the course start date, you are eligible for a 90% refund.

A 10% administrative fee will be deducted.

b. Cancellation Within 7 Days Before Start

If you cancel your enrollment within 7 days before the course start date, you are eligible for a 50% refund.

c. No Refund After Course Starts

No refund will be provided once the course has started, regardless of attendance or completion, except in exceptional cases as determined at the discretion of the management.

Cancellation Time Refund Percentage Conditions
7+ days before course start 90% 10% administrative fee deducted
Within 7 days before course start 50% No administrative fee
After course starts 0% Exceptional cases at management discretion

3. Special Circumstances

Refunds may be considered on a case-by-case basis under the following conditions:

  • Medical emergencies (documented proof required)
  • Duplicate payments

4. Refund Process

  • All refund requests must be made in writing via email to: nammacodingshaale@gmail.com
  • Include your full name, course enrolled, date of enrollment, and reason for the refund request.
  • If approved, refunds will be processed and credited 7–10 business days via the original mode of payment.

5. Non-Refundable Fees

The following are strictly non-refundable:

  • Registration fees (if separately charged)
  • Fees for completed workshops, digital materials, or one-on-one sessions
  • Payment gateway or processing fees (if applicable)
